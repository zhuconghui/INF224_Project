\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Manages multimedia objects and groups. 

This class is responsible for creating, storing, and managing various multimedia objects (like Photos, Videos, and Films) and groups of multimedia objects. It uses std\+::map to associate names with multimedia objects and groups for easy retrieval.
